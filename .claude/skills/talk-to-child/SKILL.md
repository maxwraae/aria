---
name: talk-to-child
description: Send a child back with feedback to try again. Use when a child reports
  back but the work isn't done yet — it needs more direction, a different approach,
  or corrections. Run `aria talk-to-child <id> "message"` — the child resets to
  idle, receives your feedback, and starts a new cycle. Be specific in your
  feedback — vague feedback produces vague retries. This is the iteration tool.
---
