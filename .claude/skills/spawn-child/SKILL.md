---
name: spawn-child
description: Create a child objective to handle work you can't finish this turn.
  Use when the gap to your objective is too wide to close directly — too many
  unknowns, multiple directions to explore, or a piece of work that needs its own
  cycles. Run `aria spawn-child "objective" "description" "message"` where the
  objective is what should become true (a desired state, not a task), description
  gives the child context, and message triggers its first cycle. You will judge
  the child when it reports back. Always prefer doing work yourself if you can
  close the gap this turn — a child costs at minimum three cycles.
---
