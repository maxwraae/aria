---
name: notify-max
description: Reach Max directly, outside the tree. Use when something is important
  or urgent enough that Max should know — meaningful work completed, a blocker that
  changes the plan, or something that affects other objectives. Run
  `aria notify-max "message" --important/--not-important --urgent/--not-urgent` —
  both flags are required. Important means Max needs to know. Urgent means now.
---
