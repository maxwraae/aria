---
name: resolve-child
description: Close out a child objective permanently. Use when a child reports back
  and the work is either done or the approach can't work. Run
  `aria resolve-child <id> succeed "summary"` when the desired state became true, or
  `aria resolve-child <id> fail "reason"` when the approach is fundamentally blocked.
  Both are permanent. The summary or reason becomes the permanent record. Only use
  this for terminal decisions — if the child just needs more direction, talk to it
  instead.
---
