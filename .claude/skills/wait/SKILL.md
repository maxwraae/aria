---
name: wait
description: Park yourself until something external arrives. Use when you've done
  everything you can this turn and the next step depends on someone else — Max's
  feedback, a child still working, a meeting that hasn't happened yet. Run
  `aria wait "reason"` — your status goes to idle with the reason attached. You
  stay parked until a new message arrives. If you don't call wait and don't resolve
  anything, the system marks you as stuck.
---
