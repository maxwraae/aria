# Transfer money to BoA without getting charged a fee

## Solution: Use Wise

**Problem:** BofA charges $15 for incoming SWIFT wire transfers. Revolut EU can only send to US banks via SWIFT — no workaround.

**Fix:** Route through Wise. Wise receives EUR locally (SEPA), converts at mid-market rate, and delivers USD to BofA via **domestic ACH** = $0 receiving fee.

**Flow:** Revolut EU (EUR) → Wise (SEPA, free) → converts EUR→USD → BofA (ACH, $0 fee)

**Cost:** ~€1-4 per transfer depending on amount (~0.5-0.7%) vs $15+ flat via SWIFT.
